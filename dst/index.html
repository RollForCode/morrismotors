Services

Here are just a few of the many things we cover; for anything else, just ask!

MOT

pickup and return

We can plan around you, we'll pick up your car, get it tested, and drop it back the same day. No more waiting around the MOT center.

Servicing

We offer a full range of serving levels to meet your cars needs. From oil changes, to a fiull services, and a check over the entire vehicle.

Diagnostics

Errors popping up on your Dash, or unknown noises from the rear of your car? We have the electronic and mechanical equipment to diagnose your car's problem.

Timing Belts

Timing belts need to be changed every 40-80 thousand miles, depending on the model of the car. We can replace the belt, and if required, the water pumps at the same time.

Brakes

We can work on all types of brakes, shoes, and drums, for all makes and models of car; ensuring you get optimum performance and safety.

Suspension

Springs, Dampeners, and ball joints; we can look at any suspension issues you car has to keep it working at best it possibly can.
